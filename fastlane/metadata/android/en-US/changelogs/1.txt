Initial release of Dure for Android
• Distributed e-commerce platform for small shop owners
• Zero transaction fees (0%) - unlike Shopify's 2%
• Identity management with cryptographic keys (X25519, Ed25519)
• Guest and Store Front support
• Hosting management (DNS, Database, Site deployment)
• Store management (Products, Orders, Shipments, Accounts)
• Material3 design with egui framework
• Support for Firebase, Supabase, GCE, Cafe24 VPS hosting
• Payment integration (Portone, KakaoPay)
• WebSocket-based peer-to-peer communication
• AI-ready with JSON output for automation
